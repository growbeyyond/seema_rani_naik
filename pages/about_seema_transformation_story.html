1995 - 2008

The Struggling Years: When Life Felt Ordinary

Like many of you, I spent years feeling stuck in a life that looked successful from the outside but felt empty within. I was climbing the corporate ladder, checking all the "right" boxes, yet something fundamental was missing.

The turning point came during what I now call my "dark night of the soul"—a period of deep depression and anxiety that forced me to question everything I thought I knew about success and happiness.

Key Moments from This Chapter:

  • Corporate burnout at age 28 despite external success
  • Relationship breakdown that shattered my self-worth
  • The panic attack that changed everything
"I realized that I had been living someone else's definition of success, and it was slowly killing my soul."
13

2008 - 2012

The Awakening: First Steps Toward Consciousness

My awakening didn't happen overnight. It began with a single book, a meditation class, and the courage to ask: "What if there's another way to live?" This chapter chronicles my first steps into personal development and spiritual growth.

I discovered that transformation wasn't about fixing what was broken—it was about remembering who I truly was beneath all the conditioning and expectations.

Breakthrough Moments:

  • First meditation retreat that opened my consciousness
  • Discovering my passion for holistic wellness
  • The mentor who saw my potential before I did
"The moment I stopped trying to fix myself and started accepting myself, everything began to shift."

2012 - 2017

Deep Transformation: Becoming Who I Was Meant to Be

This was the period of my deepest inner work—facing my shadows, healing generational patterns, and learning to live from my authentic self rather than my conditioned self. It was messy, beautiful, and absolutely necessary.

During this time, I also began my formal training in coaching and holistic wellness, not knowing that I was preparing for my life's work.

Transformation Milestones:

  • Completed advanced coaching certification
  • Healed my relationship with money and success
  • Created my first holistic wellness products
"True transformation happens when you stop trying to become someone else and start becoming more of who you already are."

2017 - 2020

Emerging as a Guide: Sharing the Path

As my own transformation deepened, people began asking me to share what I had learned. I started coaching informally, then formally, and discovered that guiding others on their journey was not just my calling—it was my joy.

This period also saw the birth of Twilight Organic, as I realized that true transformation requires nourishing both the inner and outer aspects of our being.

Professional Milestones:

  • Launched my first coaching program
  • Founded Twilight Organic wellness brand
  • Helped first 100 clients transform their lives
